The method

The Narrative StorySelling™ framework

Five beats you can run in any call, demo, or deck — in the order that makes a buyer sit up, lean in, and decide. Here's the shape. The depth is where the real work begins.

Beat 01 · Opportunity
Open with the possibility of a better future — before you ever mention what you sell.
Beat 02 · Problem
Name what's truly in the way, so the buyer feels understood instead of pitched.
Beat 03 · Solution
Position your product as the bridge — the obvious path from here to there.
Beat 04 · Action
Show exactly how it works, in the order that builds belief, not confusion.
Beat 05 · Transformation
Paint the "after" picture so vividly the decision feels already made.

There's a sixth element most founders never see coming — the "Guard at the Door" — that decides whether any of this matters.
